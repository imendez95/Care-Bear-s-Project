Hey there and Welcome to my page! Do you remember seeing these bears when you were a child or heard about them as you got older? If not, then let me tell you a bit about them.
They are called The Care Bears and live in a faraway place up in the clouds called Care-a-Lot. They are all a different color and have a special "belly badge" that represents their personality. The bears must stick together and work as a team as they travel around the world on missions in Caring, while evil villains try to divert their plans.
The very first Care Bears tv show came out in 1985 with only ten bears. As the years went on, more tv shows and movies were released featuring new bears with their own unique "belly badge".
